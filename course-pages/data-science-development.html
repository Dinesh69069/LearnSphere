Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a math background?

Basic high school mathematics is sufficient. We cover the necessary statistics and linear algebra concepts during the course.

What datasets will we work with?

Real-world datasets from Kaggle, UCI Machine Learning Repository, and custom datasets covering various domains like healthcare, finance, and e-commerce.

Will I learn machine learning in this course?

Yes! We cover introduction to machine learning with practical examples using Scikit-learn. For advanced ML, we recommend our Machine Learning course.

Can I use this course to land a job?

Absolutely! This course provides hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and real projects that will strengthen your data science portfolio.
