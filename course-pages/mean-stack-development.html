Frequently Asked Questions

Is MEAN stack still relevant?

Yes! MEAN stack remains popular for building scalable web applications. Many companies use it, and it provides valuable full-stack experience with JavaScript throughout.

How is MEAN different from MERN?

MEAN uses Angular for the frontend while MERN uses React. Both are excellent. This course covers MEAN; if you prefer React, check our MERN course.

Will I build complete projects?

Absolutely! You'll build multiple full-stack projects including a task management app, e-commerce platform, and real-time chat application.

Is this an online or in-person course?

This is an online course, making it flexible for working professionals. You can learn at your own pace with recorded lectures and live support sessions.
