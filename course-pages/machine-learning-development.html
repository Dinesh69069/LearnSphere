Module 1: ML Fundamentals

Feature engineering, hyperparameter tuning, and capstone projects - 2 Weeks

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need strong math skills?

Basic algebra and statistics are helpful, but we focus on practical implementation rather than heavy mathematics.

Will I learn deep learning?

This course covers ML fundamentals. Deep learning and neural networks are covered in advanced courses.

What libraries will I use?

We primarily use Scikit-learn, NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib for data processing and visualization.

Is this course self-paced?

Yes, this is an online self-paced course with recorded lectures and 24/7 support from instructors.
